# Task ID: 12

**Title:** Prepare for Launch

**Status:** pending

**Dependencies:** 11

**Priority:** high

**Description:** Finalize the app for deployment and prepare marketing materials.

**Details:**

Ensure all features are functioning as intended, prepare app store listings, and create promotional materials for launch.

**Test Strategy:**

Conduct a final review of the app and marketing materials to ensure readiness for launch.

## Subtasks

### 12.1. Conduct Final Feature Review

**Status:** pending  
**Dependencies:** None  

Review all app features to ensure they are functioning as intended before launch.

**Details:**

Test each feature of the app thoroughly to confirm that they work correctly and meet the specifications outlined in the project requirements.

### 12.2. Prepare App Store Listings

**Status:** pending  
**Dependencies:** 12.1  

Create and finalize app store listings for both iOS and Android platforms.

**Details:**

Gather all necessary information including app descriptions, screenshots, and promotional videos. Ensure compliance with app store guidelines.

### 12.3. Create Promotional Materials

**Status:** pending  
**Dependencies:** 12.1  

Design and produce marketing materials for the app launch.

**Details:**

Develop graphics, social media posts, and other promotional content to generate interest in the app prior to launch.

### 12.4. Finalize Launch Strategy

**Status:** pending  
**Dependencies:** 12.2, 12.3  

Develop a comprehensive strategy for the app launch including timelines and channels.

**Details:**

Outline the launch timeline, identify key marketing channels, and assign responsibilities to team members for execution.

### 12.5. Conduct Final Review Meeting

**Status:** pending  
**Dependencies:** 12.4  

Hold a meeting with the team to review all preparations for the app launch.

**Details:**

Discuss the status of all subtasks, address any last-minute issues, and confirm that everyone is prepared for the launch.
